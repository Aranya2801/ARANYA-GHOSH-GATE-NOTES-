About This Project

This repository is the study system I built for GATE 2026. I spent months refining it — not just the content, but the structure: how topics connect, what order to cover them in, which problems reveal the most gaps, and what a useful revision sheet actually looks like.

The notes are organized around understanding, not memorization. Every formula has a derivation. Every algorithm has a trace. Every section ends with a clear statement of what you should actually know and be able to do.

I'm releasing all of this openly because I benefited enormously from notes and resources shared by others during my preparation. This is my contribution to that tradition.

A note on strategy

I did not use every resource available. I picked a small number of sources, went deep on each, and spent most of my time solving problems and reviewing mistakes. The Resources page lists exactly what I used, with honest notes on what each resource is good and not good for.

If I were to distill preparation into one principle: understand the question behind the question. GATE tests your ability to reason under constraints, not recall under pressure.
